Dear Everyone

Over the last 2 years, I have been the sole developer of AE and AE2 and, for the most part, it has been a fun 2 years. I've met a lot of fun people, and had a lot of fun creating and maintaining two great mods.

Over the past several months, I've found that I've been having less and less fun and becoming increasingly aggravated; partially due to the increased negativity surrounding the mod, but mostly because I have simply pushed myself far too hard, for far too long. I've been the only person working on the art, code, and dealing with most support issues since the project's inception. I realize now that my mistake was never accepting help until it was too late.

Over the past 2 weeks, I've attempted to try and rectify my mistake, however its become painfully clear I was far too late in making this change and things have only snowballed drastically, instead of improving. After debating what to do and considering various options, I have come to a decision.

With a heavy heart I resign as developer of AE2. I will, over the next several weeks, be working with several people to open up the mod and put a team together to carry on at minimum maintaining the mod as I step down.

Thanks for everyone's support and encouragement these past two years, and hopefully this transition will be as smooth as possible.

Sep 23 2014 - AlgorithmX2
