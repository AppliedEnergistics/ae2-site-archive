Inscriber


The Inscriber is used to press items using various Inscriber Plates. Each operation requires 1k AE charged up. The Inscriber needs a supply of 10 AE/t and 100 ticks to fully charge up.

    automated from side with bottom press
  4. Output 
    automated from any side without press
